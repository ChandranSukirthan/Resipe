ossobuco


Ingredients


  • Veal Shanks (Ossobuco): 4 pieces, about 1 1/2 inches thick
  • All-Purpose Flour: for dredging
  • Salt and Pepper: to taste
  • Olive Oil: 1/4 cup
  • Unsalted Butter: 4 tablespoons
  • Onions: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Carrots: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Celery: 1 stalk, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Tomato Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Dry White Wine: 1 cup
  • Beef or Veal Broth: 2 cups
  • Canned Diced Tomatoes: 1 can (14 ounces)
  • Bay Leaves: 2
  • Fresh Thyme: 1 sprig


Instructions


    Prepare the Veal Shanks:

  • Dredge the veal shanks in flour, shaking off excess.
  • Season with salt and pepper.

    • Brown the Veal Shanks:

  • In a large, heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil and 2 tablespoons of butter over medium-high heat.
  • Brown the veal shanks on all sides. Remove and set aside

    • Sauté Vegetables:

  • In the same pot, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter.
  • Sauté the onions, carrots, celery, and garlic until softened.

    • Add Tomato Paste and Deglaze

  • Stir in the tomato paste and cook for a minute to enhance the flavor.
  • Pour in the white wine to deglaze the pot, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.

    • Prepare Gremolata:
